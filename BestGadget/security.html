Safe and Secure Shopping

  • Is it safe to use my credit/debit card on BestGadgets?

    Your online transaction on BestGadgets is secure with the highest levels of transaction security currently available on the Internet. BestGadgets uses 256-bit encryption technology to protect your card information while securely transmitting it to the respective banks for payment processing. All credit card and debit card payments on BestGadgets are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways managed by leading banks. Banks now use the 3D Secure password service for online transactions, providing an additional layer of security through identity verification.

  • Does BestGadgets store my credit/debit card infomation?

    BestGadgets stores the first 6 and last 4 digits of your card number in a secure and encrypted manner. The first 6 digits (also known as the Bank Identification Number) are used to identify the bank name and country where your card was issued. The first 6 and last 4 digits are together used for fraud detection and prevention purposes.
